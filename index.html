About Us

Welcome to IndoCleanAir, your trusted helpdesk and information center dedicated to addressing the critical issue of haze disasters in Indonesia. Our mission is to provide essential resources, guidance, and support to communities, organizations, and individuals affected by and concerned about the devastating impact of haze on our environment and well-being.


At the bottom right corner of our website, you'll find our friendly chatbot, a virtual assistant ready to answer your questions, provide real-time assistance, and guide you through the wealth of information and resources we offer. Whether you're seeking urgent help during a crisis or simply looking for information, our chatbot is here to make your experience on IndoCleanAir seamless and informative. Join us in our mission to combat haze disasters, and let our chatbot be your trusted companion in this important journey.
